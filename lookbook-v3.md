# Lookbook: q15 Video v3 — The Agent Is Yours

> Generated: 2026-06-23
> Theme: AI agents, memory, prompt injection, and the proxy architecture of ownership
> Visual concept: A world that warms from cold institutional blues to warm golden autonomy — the journey from being a user of platforms to owning your agent.

## Visual Concept

A world that warms from cold institutional blues to warm golden autonomy. The character moves from cold, frustrated dependence to warm, empowered ownership. Light is the primary narrative device: cold screen-glow gives way to warm desk-lamp light, which gives way to golden-hour sunlight through a window.

## Character Design — "Maya"

**Exact character description (used verbatim in EVERY prompt)**:

"a woman in her early thirties with short dark brown hair, wearing a charcoal knit sweater, sitting at a light oak wooden desk with a laptop, slim build, fair skin"

**Character consistency rules**:
- Same hair, same sweater, same desk in every image
- Only the emotional expression, lighting, and composition change
- The desk and laptop are constant anchors
- She is always alone in the frame (no other people)

## Style Modifier (appended to EVERY prompt)

### Color Progression Arc

- Opening to Discovery: cold blue-gray yields to amber desk-lamp warmth — the thaw begins
- Discovery to Attack: warming amber is interrupted by red-tinged hard light — danger enters
- Attack to Resolution: red danger light clears to warm golden-hour light — safety and ownership

## Visual Motifs

### Motif 1: Light through a window
- Opening: cold distant blue light from a window behind her, barely visible
- Attack: window light gone, replaced by harsh screen glow
- Resolution: warm golden-hour light flooding through the window, dominant

### Motif 2: The laptop screen
- Opening: cold cyan glow, the amnesiac AI
- Discovery: screen showing code/memory, warmer glow
- Attack: screen showing malicious text, red-tinted
- Resolution: screen showing peaceful work, warm glow

### Motif 3: The desk lamp
- Opening: off — only cold screen light
- Discovery: turned on — warm amber pool of light
- Attack: flickering or harsh — tension
- Resolution: on, steady, warm — stability
